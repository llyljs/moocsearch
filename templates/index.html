Road map

Our plan to built this amazing application

  • March 2018

    We got the idea!

    When learning on the Internet, we often get frustrated if we want to download the learning materials. And it's annoying when we find a lecture interesting but has closed by the teacher. The Mooc websites just has so many restrictions that make us unhappy. So this is how we got the idea. We are going to make MOOC great again!

  • April 2018

    We built a Website

    To make people better understand our work, we built this website as well as some unfinished functions for further development.

  • May 2018

    Get our App runing

    We plan to deploy our app to 'pythonanywhere', so you can use our app online. But we will also provide commandline tool for you to download for better experice.

  • June 2018

    Optimize our App

    At that time, we decided to optimize our app including accelerating speed, bug fixing, as well as adding some new features.
